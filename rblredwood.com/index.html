ABOUT US

Over 30 years of experience

RBL Redwood was founded in 2013, we specialize in delivering professional design services tailored to the construction industry. Our team of experienced engineers is equipped to handle projects ranging from minor highway improvements to larger Section 38/278 highway schemes. We take pride in staying up-to-date with the latest planning and design standards, including DMRB, Manual for Streets, and Manual for Streets 2. By utilizing cutting-edge design software and applications, we ensure that every project is executed with the highest level of precision and accuracy. From the early stages of the design process, we focus on identifying the most efficient and cost-effective approach, ensuring you benefit from both optimal design solutions and significant cost savings. Our commitment to excellence guarantees a first-class service that consistently meets and exceeds our clients' expectations.
